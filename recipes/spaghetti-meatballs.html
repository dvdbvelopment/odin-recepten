  2. Prepare the meatballs:
    • Preheat your oven to the temperature indicated in your recipe (usually around 375°F or 190°C).
  3. Layering:
    • In a baking dish, start by spreading a thin layer of tomato sauce at the bottom.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping.
    • Spread a layer of ricotta cheese over the noodles. You can also mix in chopped spinach or other herbs if desired.
    • Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the ricotta.
  4. Baking:
    • Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil to prevent excessive browning. This will also help the lasagna cook evenly.
    • Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
